Our Services
and what we can do to grow your brand awareness and social engagement
Social Media growth
There are millions of people out there who would love to hear from you. We identify them for you on the main social media platforms and bring their attention to your brand. If they like your content, they should follow you.

Our Social Media Growth team has been nurturing communities since 2011. We have proven expertise, very powerful engagement tactics and cutting edge technologies to make sure your social media accounts grow very fast with real and active followers.
Influencer Marketing
Our team helps brands engage and activate global or ultra-local audiences through Digital Influencers. We design and execute marketing strategies to amplify your brand awareness and reach new clients.

We identify top social media influencers for your industry and audit their communities to make sure they are engaged and healthy before managing the relationship with them for you.
Paid Social Media Advertising
Paid Advertising on Social Media platforms is the most cost-effective et powerful way to drive instant traffic to your websites, your products, your stores or your social media accounts.

We create the messages and track the results of our clients' advertising campaigns on Google, Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat and Twitter... and we manage for them the continuously growing budgets they invest to generate gigantic amounts of leads.
Content Marketing Creation & Story Telling
Storytelling and content marketing go hand in hand. Customers and fans want to be inspired by the brands they love. They want to be entertained, get value and feel comfortable with who you really are.

We manage your Social Media presence and create inspiring content that tells your story, hit your customers' emotional chord and makes your brand's personality shine. Great storytelling strengthens your clients and fans' loyalty and eventually generate leads and sales. 
Direct Message campaigns
It is proven that sending offers to your prospects and clients generates leads and sales. We design and execute creative strategies to bring you in front of your customers' eyes via Email, SMS, Twitter & Instagram Direct Messages, Messenger, Whatsapp... 

We don't send simple messages. We create scenarios that trigger depending on your customers' reactions. We analyze the results we get from the first campaigns and adjust our targeting and retargeting strategies. Our actions are 80% data-driven.
Consulting, Strategy & Social Media Coaching
Because some of you just need a little help to put their Social Media on tracks, we offer consulting services when you want to fix a problem, social media strategy services when you need a clear path to achieve your goals and coaching services if you want guidance and assistance on a more regular basis.

It is an affordable way for you to pick our brains and benefit from our experience while staying hands-on and doing things by yourself. Doesn't matter if you just need a simple consultation or long term assistance, we are happy to tell you everything we know.

We created Digidust back in the days because we thought it was more exciting to help many clients grow their business than focus on our product.
